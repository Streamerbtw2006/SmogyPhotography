About On the Farm

On the Farm was taken on our fellow photographer's family farm. It is where she lived for many years, and she knew it really well. On a visit back to the farm, she decided to take pictures of all the little things she loved. These pictures really convey these things.

How We Did It

The photographer had her mom pick up some grains so she could take an artistic picture of it. This is by far her favorite picture of the entire collection.
