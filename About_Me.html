About Me

My name is Amogh Babu

I'm a dreamer, an artist, and passionate about whatever I do. Growing up as a child, photography was my one interest. Being able to capture things, memories, feelings, into a still photograph was mindblowing to me, and it still is today. A good photographer strives to capture all those things and many more. I have spent almost all my life trying to perfect my photography skills. My friends and clients have told me I can capture the smells and sounds through the picture. I started photography when I was young, and I will never stop doing it. Beyond photography, I love being active. I am really social and like interacing with people, so if you see me, feel free to wave hi!

My Hobbies:

When I'm not taking pictures, I'm playing my favorite sport, Soccer. I have been playing it since I was very young, and I'm decently good at it. My friends can stil beat me easily

I played on many soccer teams in my youth, and in my school years. I still enjoy getting out on the field and kicking the ball around

Video Games. Yes, I play video games. Sometimes I just want to relax and unwind, and video games is sometimes my outlet for that. I love all types of games, and I grew up with them.

My favorite games of all time would definetly be the Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and Spider Man. I also love playing with my friends!

I love to program. I am a coder by heart, and I know a lot of it. If I couldn't make it as a photographer, I knew I had a backup of being a programmer. I know all types of languages.

The languages I am most proficient with are python and java, and it is where I do a lot of my coding. I love making things with my hands, and this is how I do it!
